What do you call the prettiest girl in the world?


From sunrise light to evening’s hue,

Every moment’s magic when I’m with you.

Your smile’s the spark that lights my way,

Even when you tell jokes that make me sway.

You’re beauty and chaos, soft and bold,

With that wicked mind and heart of gold.

You tease, you flirt, you crack those lines,

Got me blushing like it’s Valentine’s.

And through it all — wild, sweet, or deep —

You’re the love I’ll always keep.
